Simple Macroni and Cheese

Quick,easy and tasty macroni and cheese dish. Fancy designer mac and cheese often costs forty or fifty dollars to prepare when you have so many exotic cheeses, but they aren't always the best tasting. This simple recipe is cheap and tasty.

Ingredients for Mac and Cheese

  • 1 box of macroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese

How to make it

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil.
  2. To make the roux, start by melting butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, pepper and stir until smooth.
  3. Add Cheese and Macroni.
